Hey Gitcoiners,

The Sustain Web3 virtual hackathon goes live in one week! You can register via the onboarding page to receive email updates. The prize explorer will go up at midnight UTC on January 29th. Sponsors include FOAM, Xpring, and Dfuse, with more to be announced in the next week.
Register for Sustain Web3
This 2-week virtual hackathon will lead up to the Sustain Web3 physical conference in Denver on 2/13, during BUIDL Week and a day before ETHDenver. We’ll be hosting talks, workshops, and panels with leaders in the OSS ecosystem, and will showcase some hackathon projects on stage.

Join in person if you’re attending ETHDenver, or Livestream the event at home from the Gitcoin Youtube. You can sign up to attend in person here.

To learn more about what Gitcoin is doing at ETHDenver and Sustain Web3, check out our most recent blog post. We're looking forward to another great virtual hackathon with your participation!

~Gitcoin Team
