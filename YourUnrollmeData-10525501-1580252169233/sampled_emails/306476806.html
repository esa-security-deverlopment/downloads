This popular event is now available on-demand. Don’t miss out on this expert-led webcast discussing real-world evidence for new and emerging GLP-1RAs. The webcast includes case-based presentations providing evidence from around the world, including Asia, Europe and North America.

Watch the webcast

Following this webcast, you will be able to:

·      Demonstrate an increase in knowledge of the current and emerging landscape of GLP-1RAs in the context of pharmacodynamic profile, clinical use and current guidelines

·      Compare the efficacy and safety profiles of GLP-1RAs and SGLT2s

·      Implement this newly acquired knowledge in clinical decision making, selecting the right treatment at the right time for the patient based on local healthcare settings
