Make sense of your threat landscape through the eyes of a hacker. Save Me a Spot
 
 
 
 
Cybersecurity is racing towards a future of artificial intelligence and automation, but there's just one problem: technology still can't outwit the human creativity of a hacker. As the enterprise becomes increasingly reliant on data, hackers use a variety of methods to enter your systems undetected and extract valuable information. In this session, you'll meet the Aussie that went from hacking by the beach to becoming Global Head of Security Operations as he explores all of the things scanners can’t catch that people can!
