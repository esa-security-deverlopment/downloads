Agile Transformation:
The good, the bad, the ugly, and a plan to make it work
Register Today

"Agile transformation is a cultural challenge." We've all heard it, and we usually agree. Do we really understand what it means? Do we even know what we're after? And how can we move from Agile-ish project management to a full, enterprise program that delivers higher-quality software faster without sacrificing visibility or predictability? 

Join us on January 23rd for an informative discussion on the challenges, failures, and wins of Agile and DevOps journeys and implementation.
