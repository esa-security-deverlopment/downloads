The IdeaExchange is your home base for sharing ideas for product improvements or new features with the Trailblazer Community and Salesforce product managers. Throughout the last year, many of you have helped us reimagine the IdeaExchange and pilot a new experience: Prioritization. This process allows you to influence the product roadmap by championing the ideas that matter most to you.

Today, we’re excited to announce winning ideas from the September 2019 pilot cycle of Prioritization that are now in development. Continue to shape Salesforce products by participating in the upcoming cycle of Prioritization open January 20 through February 3, 2020.

Analytics Cloud Winner: Auto Add New Custom Fields to Report Types

A new Custom Field will automatically get added to all associated Report types. User will be notified if a Field couldn't be added to a specific Report type.

Estimated GA Release: Spring ’21

See The Idea
 
 

Sales Cloud Winner: Link Campaigns Directly to Accounts

Provide the ability to relate an Account to a Campaign, whereas today you can relate a Lead or Contact to a Campaign through Campaign Members.

Estimated GA Release: Spring ’21

See The Idea
 
 

Search Winner: Admin Control Over Search Top Results

Allow Admins to include results from up to 3 specific Objects in the top search results. Display Order of Objects will continue to be informed by machine learning.

Estimated GA Release: Spring ’21
