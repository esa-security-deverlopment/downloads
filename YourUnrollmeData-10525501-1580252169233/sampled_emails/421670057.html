We hope you've had time to explore the Mobile Fax feature in the HP Smart app. As you continue to familiarize yourself with the feature, here are some helpful tips.

 ‌
 

Contacts:

Save your contacts to make sure you always have their information on hand. Their information will be ready the next time you need to send a fax to them.
 

Sender Profiles:

Create a profile including your name and contact information. This will be ready the next time you send a fax.‌
 

Cover Pages:

Once you create a cover page, you can save it so it’s ready for the next time you need to send a fax. Simply edit it as necessary, so it's always ready to use.
