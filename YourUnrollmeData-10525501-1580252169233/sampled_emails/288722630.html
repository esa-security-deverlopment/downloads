Farm Credit Administration posts proposed rule and board policy statement

A proposed rule (PDF) was published in the Federal Register today on amortization limits. The comment period ends on March 23. To comment, go to comment form, click inside the Regulation field, and select the proposed rule from the dropdown menu.

In addition, the FCA board issued a policy statement: Oversight of the Federal Agricultural Mortgage Corporation (Word).

About FCA

FCA's mission is to ensure that Farm Credit System institutions and Farmer Mac are safe, sound, and dependable sources of credit and related services for all creditworthy and eligible persons in agriculture and rural America. Our agency was created by a 1933 executive order of President Franklin D. Roosevelt. Today the agency derives its authority from the Farm Credit Act of 1971, as amended.
