Esta Noche No Dormir - Resident Night l 31.01.20

LineUp-Alphabetic Order

ABSTRS (ENND/SLR/Tekla)

Gothenburg based ’Abstrs’ is originally from Stockholm and one of the co-founders and residents of underground collectives Tekla, Outer Space and OBLNS, as well as Harbour. Abstrs delivers sets combining percussive industrial sounds with driven acid and he just recently released his first solo EP ’Reliquary’ on the infamous Stockholm imprint Street Life Rhythm, as well as a collab with Just En on Lyon based Lett Records.

https://soundcloud.com/abstrstekla

Adam Deez (ENND)

DJ involved in one of Gothenburg’s most active underground collectives.

https://soundcloud.com/adamdeez

bSMNT (ENND)

bSMNT started his DJ carreer 10 years ago in a city just outside Gothenburg.
With over 500 gigs in is luggage bSMNT will guarantee every set to be satisfactory for your ears.
When playing techno, bSMNT will usually lean on the more melodic techno with pumping basslines.

SoniC (ENND/STRECK/Magazin)

SoniC, the stray dog of Gothenburgs upcoming underground scene. He started as a promoter and event manager who evolved him to run the venue M3 (Magnus & Magnus). He build it up as one of Gothenburg’s most pushing and exploring electronic music spot. During 2019 he start to play around different DJ both in Gothenburg and other cities in Sweden. He also started No Regrets with focus on bigger touring acts as Ellen Alien & Pleasurkraft. A continued flow with the concept Magazin who got further developed to Esta Noche, No Dormir & STRECK.

https://soundcloud.com/newsonicmusic
