Revenue operations (or RevOps) is likely not an unfamiliar term to you. As it grows in popularity, so does its use in blogs, conference talks and on social media. But is it just a buzzword? Or something more?


In this first episode of RevOps and Hops, Chargify co-founder and CTO, Michael Klett, and ProfitWell founder and CEO, Patrick Campbell, are joined by Proof co-founder and CEO, Dave Rogenmoser to demystify revenue operations: 

  • RevOps is more than just a buzzword. It’s a major industry shift happening right beneath our feet. 
  • RevOps is synonymous with an enterprise-wide focus on revenue as a key metric – and its success lies in rallying the entire organization around this metric. 
  • While RevOps sounds great in theory, it’s much more difficult to put into practice. The guests discuss how they’ve seen RevOps grow and manifest in the SaaS organizations we know and love today.
