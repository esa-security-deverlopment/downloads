What is the iOS 5 users for free promotional plan?

The iOS 5 users for free Jira Software Cloud subscription is a limited but a long-term promotion for new users signing up with Jira Software from the Jira Cloud app downloaded on App Store on iOS devices. The iOS 5 users for free promotional subscription only applies to the Jira Software Cloud product, not any other Atlassian products. 

The iOS 5 users for free promotional subscription began on September 1, 2018 and will end in January 31, 2020. The iOS 5 users for free promotional subscription entitles new users a free Jira Software Cloud subscription for up to 5 users.

I have entered my payment details and have been charged for a Jira Software Cloud subscription, does this apply to me?

What is going to happen to my subscription?

You have a choice between the paid STANDARD PLAN or the official FREE PLAN for Jira Software Cloud on January 31, 2020.

  1. Before January 31, 2020, the promotional subscription still applies and no subscription plan changes will occur. You may see STANDARD PLAN in your account but the promotional subscription still applies and you are still entitled to free use of Jira Software Cloud if you have 5 or less users added in the product.

  2. When the offer ends in January 31, 2020, you will be automatically moved to the Jira Software Cloud STANDARD PLAN. In order to keep using Jira Software Cloud on the STANDARD PLAN, you will have to enter payment details within 28 days to keep using the product. If no payment details are entered, the plan will try to automatically downgrade you to the FREE PLAN.

  3. At any time before or after January 31, 2020 you can move to the FREE PLAN immediately if you do not wish to go on a paid subscription.

What are the differences between the FREE PLAN and STANDARD PLAN?

Note that if you take no action, you will be automatically switched to the STANDARD PLAN at January 31, 2020. However, at any point in time you can always change to the FREE PLAN at no cost. We do not alter or remove any Jira Software Cloud configuration or data if you change from your existing plan to a new plan.

How do I change my plan to FREE PLAN or STANDARD PLAN?

  1. You need to have site administrator permissions. If you are not a site administrator, contact your administrator to give you site administrator privileges or get your site administrator change your plan.

  2. Go to admin.atlassian.com and login with your Atlassian account.

  3. Select your Jira instance, firewicca

  4. Under the “Subscriptions & Billing” there will be a “Manage subscriptions” section. Clicking on “Manage subscription” will show all the products you’ve subscribed to, including Jira Software Cloud. You’ll be able to see your active Jira Software Cloud subscription. From here you’ll be able to switch the PLAN between the FREE PLAN or STANDARD PLAN. For more instructions on switching plans see here: Manage your product plans

  5. If you switch to the STANDARD Plan, you will need to enter your payment details to keep using Jira Software if you haven't yet. For instructions on entering payment details, see here: Enter payment details and manage your bill

  6. If you switch to the FREE PLAN, you MUST have 10 or less users in the Jira Software instance. If you have more than 10 users and you are switching to the FREE plan, remove users until you have 10 or less. For instructions on how to remove users, see here: Removing users

How do I calculate the pricing for the paid STANDARD PLAN?

  1. Go to: Jira Cloud pricing calculator

  2. Select the plan type - FREE or STANDARD

  3. Enter the number of users

  4. The monthly pricing summary will appear automatically
