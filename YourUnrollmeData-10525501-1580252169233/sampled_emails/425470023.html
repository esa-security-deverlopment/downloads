If you don’t clear your to-do list each day, you’re far from alone. A survey by the time-tracking app RescueTime found that 26% of respondents leave the office having accomplished less than intended. Why? “A correlation between workplace culture and distraction,” says Nir Eyal, author of Indistractable: How to Control Your Intention and Choose Your Life. So if your resolution is to be more productive in 2020, try Eyal’s tips for limiting distractions—what he calls the skill of the century.

Nir Eyal on building “indistractable” teams
